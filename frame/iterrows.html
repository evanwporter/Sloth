Frame.iterrows¶

Frame.iterrows()¶

Iterate over the rows of the frame.

Yields¶

np.ndarray

Numpy array representing a row of the frame.

Examples¶

>>> frame = Frame(np.array([[1, 2, 3], [4, 5, 6]]))
>>> for row in frame.iterrows():
...     print(row)
[1 2 3]
[4 5 6]
